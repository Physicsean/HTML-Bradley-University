Located in sunny Sommerville Massachusetts, Bradley University was established in 2018 to provide access to higher education in math and english. With a student to faculty ratio of 5 to 1, we insure that everyone gets enough attention to succeed in their discipline We've become well known in the Boston area, with many graduates going on to advanced degrees from other Massachusetts based colleges, or into industry where they help in progressing research in their fields.
