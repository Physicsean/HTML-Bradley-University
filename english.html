Our Englih department focuses on great literature from countries and cultures around the world, as well as classes on imprving your grammer and vocabulary. We specialize in American accent english, but we also have electives in British, Australian, and Canadien accents. We also offer study abroad in other English speaking countries so you can speak and be undewrstood no matter where you practice speaking. In order to graduate, you need to complete 30 of the 35 credits offered. In order to graduate with honors, you need to complete all 35 credits with a GPA of 3.0 or higher.
