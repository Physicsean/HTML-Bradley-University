Join Student Goverment

The Student Goverment association is a great way to enter a carerr in politics. Manage clubs and funds, and meet high level executives in the school and local political world. Many people in this club go on to serve on the Sommerville boards, and we even boast 3 alummni who later became Aldermen.


Play Ultimate

Ultimate Frissbee club is where its at to make new Friends. We are second in the league, but with your help we are sure to improve to first. Come toss the old discus, and if ultamate isnt your thing, we also offer a large frisbee golf course. Stretching over 4 miles of open and ruggen terrain, we take all experience levels, and will make sure you leave a pro.

Be part of your community

Like Volunteering. Local organizations need your help to stay afloat. Help feed the homeless, cloth the poor and fight diseases in your local community. We have an in house soup kitchen, where you can serve and interact with the local community. We also give rides out to the locol co-op farm, where we grow food for local green organizations.
