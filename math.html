We focus on a well rounded math education to insure our graduates leave with the skills to do math in any discipline. WE are nationally ranked in chaos theory and harmonic analysis. Our thought provoking programs will stretch your mind to new heights, and after you graduate, you will be well equiped to solve any problem that comes your way. In order to graduate, you need to complete 30 of the 35 credits offered. In order to graduate with honors, you need to complete all 35 credits with a GPA of 3.0 or higher.
