Finacial Aid

We are very flexible when it comes to payment plans for our university. We are so proud of our education, we will even bet on your success. With our 10/10 plan, you'll pay us 10% of what you make for the first 10 years after college. When you succeed, we succeed. We also offer other payment plans for 3, 5 or 10 year repayment. We also offer need based scholarships to students who need it.


Student Services

Feeling sick. Need help buying textbooks. Need Therapy. We have your answers. Stop by our services booth in the student Union and we can help you get on the right track to student success. Our medical services are top in the nation, and be it a mental or physical problem, we can cure it. We have experts in depression, stress, family planning, and relationship counseling. Stop by today!

Tutoring

We offer tutoring in all subjects every Monday through Friday at the Library. Set up an appointment online, and we will set you up wth a tutor in your subject to gear you up to passing those exams. Spots fill up quick, so book today. All of our tutors are certified by the class proffessor before they can help you in your subject, so you know you are getting the best help possible for the course you are taking. If you would like to apply to be a tutor, you must pass the course with a grade of B+ or higher, and then speak with your professor.
